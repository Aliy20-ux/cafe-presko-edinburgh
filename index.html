Our Story

Where two worlds
come together

Cafe Presko was born from a simple truth: the best food carries the warmth of the people who make it. Nestled on Gorgie Road, we bring the bold, vibrant spirit of Filipino cuisine face-to-face with Edinburgh's own soulful character — creating a dining experience that is wholly unique, deeply comforting, and impossible to forget. Every burger is a love letter. Every dessert, a celebration. Pull up a chair.
